Entrepreneurship CELL

Centre for Entrepreneurship, Faculty of Engineering CHRIST UNIVERSITY would be the hub of entrepreneurial activity at Kengeri Campus. It is proposed to bring incubated start-ups, co-working ventures, industry mentors, eminent academicians and researchers together at our centre. This unique ecosystem thrives on continuous interaction between theory and practice, and between Academia & Industry. Further stimulus comes from our curated events and tailored modules on Entrepreneurship. The Centre would network with Industry, mentors, Investors & Organisations to drive this effort. Promising early stage ventures may get access to seed funding and distinguished mentors with years of industry experience
